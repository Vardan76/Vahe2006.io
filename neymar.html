NEYMAR JR

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team. A prolific goalscorer and renowned playmaker, he is regarded as one of the best players in the world, as well as one of the greatest Brazilian footballers of all time. Neymar has scored at least 100 goals for three different clubs, making him one of three players to achieve this.

Neymar came into prominence at Santos, where he made his professional debut aged 17. He helped the club win two successive Campeonato Paulista championships, a Copa do Brasil, and the 2011 Copa Libertadores; the latter being Santos' first since 1963. Neymar was twice named the South American Footballer of the Year, in 2011 and 2012, and soon relocated to Europe to join Barcelona. As part of Barcelona's attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez, dubbed MSN, he won the continental treble of La Liga, the Copa del Rey, and the UEFA Champions League. He then attained a domestic double in the 2015–16 season. Motivated to be a talisman at club level, Neymar transferred to PSG in 2017[7] in a move worth €222 million, making him the most expensive player ever.[note 1] In France, he won four league titles, among other honours, and was voted Ligue 1 Player of the Year in his debut season.[10] Notably, he helped PSG attain a domestic quadruple in the 2019–20 season, and led the club to its first ever Champions League Final.


Early life

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior was born in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, to Neymar Santos Sr. and Nadine da Silva and had a Christian upbringing.[14][15] He inherited his name from his father, who is a former footballer and became his son's advisor as Neymar's talents began to grow.[16] Neymar comments on his father's role: "My father has been by my side since I was little. He takes care of things, my finances and my family." Growing up, Neymar combined his love of futsal with street football.[18] Neymar said that futsal had a massive influence on him growing up, helping him develop his technique, speed of thought and ability to perform moves in tight spaces.[19]

2003, Neymar moved with his family to São Vicente, where he began playing for youth side Portuguesa Santista.[20] Then, later in 2003, they moved to Santos, where Neymar joined Santos.[21] With the success of his youth career and added income, the family bought their first property, a house next to Vila Belmiro, Santos' home stadium. Their quality of family life improved, as at age 15, Neymar was earning 10,000 reais per month and at 16, 125,000 reais per month. At 17, he signed his first full professional contract, was upgraded to the Santos first team, and began signing his first sponsorship deals

    FACTS ABOUT NEYMAR
  1. Debut:
    • He made his professional debut for Santos in 2009 at the age of 17 and quickly established himself as one of the most promising young talents in Brazilian football. He helped Santos win the Copa do Brasil in 2010 and the Copa Libertadores in 2011, scoring crucial goals in both tournaments.
  2. Debut in International team:
    • In 2011, Neymar made his debut for the Brazilian national team and quickly became one of the team's key players. He helped the team win the Confederations Cup in 2013 and the gold medal at the 2016 Summer Olympics. Neymar's performances for Santos and the Brazilian national team attracted the attention of top European clubs, and he eventually joined FC Barcelona in 2013.
  3. Trio MSN:
    • He formed a lethal attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suarez at Barcelona, winning multiple domestic and international titles, including the Uefa Champions League in 2015.
  4. Record-breaking transfer:
    • In 2017, Neymar made a record-breaking transfer to Paris Saint-Germain for €222 million, making him the most expensive player in football history at the time. He has continued to be a prolific scorer and creative force in attack for both PSG and the Brazilian national team, and he remains one of the most popular and recognizable footballers in the world.
  5. 100th professional goal:
    • Neymar scored his 100th professional goal for Santos on his 20th birthday in a league clash against Palmeiras. Unfortunately, his club lost the match 2-1..
  6. Followers:
    • With over 200 million followers on Instagram, Neymar is the third most followed footballer on the platform.
